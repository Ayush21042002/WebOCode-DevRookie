    The Call of Duty World League and Call of Duty Pro League both bring together professional players to compete and climb the international ladder. Only the most elite players reach the Call of Duty Championship, which has been the game's highest level since 2013.

    Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Major Championships

    Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Major Championships is among the most venerated competitive gaming tournaments. Known colloquially as simply the Majors, this tournament takes place twice a year and has prize money reaching $1 million

    FIFA eWorld Cup

    Since 2004, gamers have gathered for the FIFA eWorld Cup to determine the top (virtual) footballer in the world. Forget broadcasts on YouTube and Twitch, this tournament is so big that it is actually broadcast on television in many countries around the world.

    League Of Legends World Championships

    For the teams competing in the League of Legends World Championships every year they first have to make their way through regional competitions to even get an invitation, and once they're in, it's high-level play for $1 million and the 70-pound Summoner's Cup trophy.

    Pokemon World Championships

    Play! Pokemon tournaments start at the local level and end with the invite-only Pokemon World Championships for the best of the best. Players compete against their peers in the trading card game and video game series for scholarships and other prizes.

    What is Esports?

    Esports (also known as electronic sports, e-sports, or eSports) is a form of competition using video games.

    Esports often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams.
